Alarm Connection Instructions

CAUTION: Installation must be performed with the negative battery terminal disconnected. Incorrect connection may damage the motorcycle's electronics or the alarm module.

App Pairing

For the first connection with the mobile app via Bluetooth, a unique logKey is required, which can be found on the alarm housing:

logKey

Next, enter the default PIN 1234. You should set your own PIN code in the application afterwards.

Installation Tips

  • Mounting Location: Do not mount the alarm directly under metal frame elements to avoid limiting the range of the GSM and Bluetooth modules. The best location is the space under the seat or the side fairing.
  • Fuse: It is recommended to use a 10A fuse on the red wire (+12V).
  • VERY IMPORTANT:

    Horn:     In most motorcycle electrical systems, +12V is supplied after the ignition is turned on, and the horn is controlled by supplying ground via the handlebar switch. To use the motorcycle's factory horn, you must use a relay or install an additional dedicated horn.

    Turn Signals: When connecting the turn signals, ensure you are connecting to the 12V power wire. Accidentally connecting to ground will damage the alarm!
